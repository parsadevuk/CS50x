What I Do

I specialise in three core areas:

Building web and mobile applications

From initial concept through to deployment, I create full-stack solutions using modern technologies.

AI and automation

I help businesses integrate artificial intelligence into their operations, focusing on practical applications rather than buzzwords.

Solution engineering

I don't just write code; I design complete systems that make sense for your users and your business goals.

My approach is straightforward: understand the problem properly, design a clear solution, and build it to last. I care about user experience and interface design because an application that's difficult to use is an application that fails, regardless of how clever the code might be.
